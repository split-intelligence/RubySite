Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how RubyTech Learning collects, uses, stores and protects your personal information.

Last Updated: {{ today|default:"July 2026" }}

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information including:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Student identification details
  • Payment records
  • Course enrollment history
  • Device and browser information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your student account.
  • Process course registrations and payments.
  • Provide course materials and certificates.
  • Send important notifications and updates.
  • Improve our educational platform and services.
  • Provide customer support.

3. Data Protection

RubyTech Learning implements reasonable administrative, technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration or destruction.

4. Payment Information

Payments are processed through trusted third-party payment providers. RubyTech Learning does not store your debit, credit card or banking credentials on its servers.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited information only when necessary to:

  • Process payments.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Protect the security of our platform.
  • Provide services through trusted technology partners.

6. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, improve functionality and analyze platform usage for a better learning experience.

7. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Update your profile.
  • Request deletion of your account where permitted by law.
  • Withdraw consent for certain communications.

8. Data Retention

Student records and payment information are retained only for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through the platform or by email where appropriate.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please contact the RubyTech Learning support team.

Privacy Commitment

RubyTech Learning is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled responsibly, securely and transparently.
